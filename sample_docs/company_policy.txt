Acme Corp Remote Work Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their probationary period (90 days) are eligible for remote work. Part-time employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

2. Core Hours
Remote employees must be available during core hours: 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in their local timezone. Meetings scheduled outside core hours require 48-hour notice.

3. Equipment
Acme Corp provides a laptop, monitor, and $500 annual stipend for home office equipment. Employees must return all company equipment within 5 business days of separation.

4. Data Security
Employees must use the company VPN when accessing internal systems. Personal devices may not store confidential customer data. Violations may result in disciplinary action.

5. Expense Reimbursement
Internet costs up to $75/month are reimbursable with manager approval. Coworking space memberships are not covered unless pre-approved by HR.

6. Performance Reviews
Remote employees are evaluated on the same criteria as in-office staff. Annual reviews occur in March. Mid-year check-ins are held in September.

Contact: hr@acmecorp.com for questions about this policy.
